# Design Reference: Equinox Group Fitness Page

Source: https://www.equinox.com/groupfitness

## Aesthetic
Minimalist, high-contrast luxury fitness branding. Predominantly white/neutral backgrounds with black text, sparing use of color, generous whitespace as the primary visual device (not color or decoration).

## Typography
- Bold, uppercase, wide-letter-spacing sans-serif headlines (tagline-style: "DESIGNED FOR THE INDIVIDUAL. POWERED BY THE COLLECTIVE.")
- Strong size contrast between hero/section headlines and body copy
- Consistent font weight used to delineate section headers from content

## Navigation
- Horizontal top nav: Clubs, Membership, Classes, Personal Training, Pilates, Spa, More
- Flat text links, no heavy button styling in the nav itself

## Imagery
- Square product/class photography (consistent ~508x508px treatment) for class modules (e.g. Precision Ride, Sculpted Yoga, Pilates Rise)
- Larger rectangular imagery for feature sections (Unlimited Signature Classes, Expert Instructors)
- Consistent visual treatment across all images — no mixed aspect ratios

## Buttons / CTAs
- Plain text CTAs ("Learn More", "Take A Class") rather than heavy filled buttons
- Emphasis on typography/content over visual button flourish
- Where filled buttons are used, they are solid black or solid white with sharp (non-rounded or minimally-rounded) corners

## Category Navigation
- Class categories (Boxing, Strength, HIIT, etc.) shown as a single horizontal row of clickable text tags — flat, no heavy chip backgrounds, separated by spacing rather than borders

## Layout / Spacing
- Generous whitespace between major sections, used deliberately to pace the user through content sequentially
- Three feature blocks for exclusive class types, each get dedicated full-width treatment
- FAQ section at the bottom in a simple accordion/list style

## Takeaways for our app redesign
- Switch from dark navy/orange "gym app" theme to a light, high-contrast, editorial look: white background, black text, minimal single accent color
- Bold uppercase headers for section titles ("TODAY'S SESSION", "CHOOSE YOUR FOCUS")
- Replace rounded colorful pill chips with flat, underline-on-select text tags
- Buttons become solid black with white uppercase text, sharp corners, no gradients
- Increase whitespace/padding throughout; remove card drop-shadows and gradients in favor of thin hairline dividers
- Keep imagery-style treatment for category chips minimal since we don't have photography — use clean iconography or none at all, leaning on typography
